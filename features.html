Key Features of Smart House

• Remote Control: Operate lights, fans, AC, and appliances from anywhere using your phone.
• Voice Control: Compatible with Alexa, Google Assistant, and Siri.
• Automation: Set schedules and scenes like “Good Morning” or “Movie Time”.
• Energy Monitoring: Track usage in real-time and reduce electricity bills.
• Security: Smart locks, cameras, motion sensors, and instant mobile alerts.
• Universal Hub: Works with Wi-Fi, Zigbee, Z-Wave, and all major brands.
• Easy Expansion: Add new devices anytime without changing the system.
