Benefits of Smart House

• Convenience: Control all devices from one app — no need to walk around.
• Energy Savings: Reduce electricity bills by up to 30% with smart monitoring.
• Enhanced Security: Get instant alerts on your phone for any unusual activity.
• Comfort: Lights, temperature, and music adjust automatically to your routine.
• Peace of Mind: Check cameras and lock doors remotely, even when you're away.
• Future-Proof: Easily upgrade and expand as new smart tech arrives.
• Accessibility: Voice control helps elderly and differently-abled users.
